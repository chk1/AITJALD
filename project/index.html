M端nster house hold data visualized

This tool visualizes M端nster's household counts. On the map to the right you will see the household counts for each district, there are 45 lower districts (Stadtteile) and 6 upper districts (Stadtbezirke) in total. Below there are 10 datasets to chose from, where you can select to view the distribution of households by the number of occupants, by age or gender.

Move your mouse over a district or click on one to retrieve more statistical data.
